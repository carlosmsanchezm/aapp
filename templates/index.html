So You Want to be Woke is a site meant to help you become more aware of the issues facing different marginalized communities. Some of these topics may be ones you are aware of and others not. By making this site, we hope to make it easier for you to gain an initial understanding of the different ways people may be targeted for their physical features, beliefs, or characteristics.

How do I contribute?

We are always looking for new content to share on this site. This site accepts links to articles, books, and videos. Please only submit content that is publicly available or that you have the rights to. The team has the final decision on what content gets published to the site. Content submission or new ideas can be sent to team@soyouwanttobewoke.com and should be formatted in the following format:

  • Name of Content
  • Format (book/article/video)
  • Content Link
  • Topic (ex. Racial identity, etc)
  • Photo/Image
  • Blurb about topic
